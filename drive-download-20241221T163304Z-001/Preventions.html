PREVENTION

If you have not been vaccinated against rabies in the past, you need 4 doses of rabies vaccine over 2 weeks (given on days 0, 3, 7, and 14).

Ask animal owners if it is okay to approach their dog. This ensures the safety and comfort of both you and the pet.

Never leave small children unattended around animals.

Don't disturb animals that are eating or sleeping.

Avoid running past dogs.

Never reach through fences.
