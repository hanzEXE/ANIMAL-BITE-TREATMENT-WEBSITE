ANIMAL BITE TREATMENT: FOR DOGS AND CATS

OVERVIEW

You'll discover step-by-step instructions for administering first aid for animal bites, prevention, as well as when to seek nearby animal bite clinics, more information about dog and cat bites, and other more related information. Our goal is to educate and guide you with the knowledge needed to successfully respond to emergency situations. We further provide a list of nearby animal bite clinics and professional information on prevention and treatment. With the help of this informational website, we can assist with the safety of the animals and the people involved.

FIRST AID FOR DOG AND CAT BITES

1

Gauge the size and depth of the wound. If the wound looks deep or severe, or is losing a large amount of blood, seek professional help immediately.

2

Use a clean cloth or towel to apply direct pressure to control bleeding.

3

Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water. If available, wear sterile gloves before touching the wound.

4

Clean the bite area with soap and warm water for five minutes. Rinse thoroughly to remove all soap.

5

Cover the bite with a clean, dry dressing. Applying antibiotic ointment is optional.
